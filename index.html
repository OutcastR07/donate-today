Donation Plans

We are taking donations to help the helpless
We are planning to move according to these charts

Food

Food will be bought and supplied to all the people in the villages from the donation money. Our first priority is the north of our country at the moment, since the people of these areas are unable to buy food for themselves cause of the heavy loss from flood.

Medicine

Emergency medicines will be supplied to all the people in need. Specially to the people who are unable to buy their own food for financial crisis.

Shelter

Houses will be built for the homeless with the donation money. It is really a nightmare when people do not have shelter for them to sleep at night. So, building shelter for the families in need are our priority.
